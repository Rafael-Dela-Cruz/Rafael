Philippine Mythological Creatures

What is Philippine Mythological Creatures?

  • In the rich tapestry of Philippine folklore, a myriad of mythological creatures populate the imaginations of more than 140 distinct ethnic groups across the archipelago. These creatures, often embodying the fears, wonders, and mysteries of the natural world, are deeply ingrained in the cultural heritage of the Philippines.
